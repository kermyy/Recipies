Chicken Rice

Description

Hainanese Chicken rice is a chicken and rice dish of lightly poached chicken, served alongside rice thatâ€™s been cooked in the poaching liquid, with ginger-garlic chilli sauce and soup made from the poaching liquid.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Ginger-garlic Chilli Sauce
  • Soup
  • Sambal Kicap
  • Ginger and Spring Onion Oil

Steps

  1. Poach the chicken.
  2. Cook the rice in chicken stock.
  3. Plate chicken on top of rice with side of chilli and the sambal kicap.
  4. Done!
