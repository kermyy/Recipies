Lasagna

Description

This is a classic homemade lasagna recipe made with layers of gooey melted mozzarella cheese, a parmesan ricotta cheese, and a robust tomato meat sauce. It is perfect for weeknight dinners, potlucks, or to take to friends who just had a baby. This lasagna recipe can be frozen so it is perfect to made ahead of time. It also makes flavorful leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Ground Beef
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Parmesan Ricotta Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce by combining ground beef, onion and marinara sauce. Salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Layer your lasagna noodles until the top of pan, adding a layer of meat sauce and cheese in between each layer.
  3. Bake in the oven for about 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
  4. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  5. Done!
